Lennart Westerlund

With more than 35 years of Lindy Hop experience, Lennart is one of very few of the pioneers that are still active in the scene. Together with many accomplishements as a dancer in the legendary troup Rhythm Hot Shots, he is creditied for bringing Frankie Manning to Herräng Dance Camp in 1989 thus playing a crucial role in the revival of the Lindy Hop. Having spent significant time with Frankie Manning, Norma Miller, Al Minns and other Savoy dancers, Lennart has his interests in the Harlem roots of the dance. Nowadays he is active as a dancer, instructor, lecturer, judge and administrator. For the first time in Bulgaria he is coming to the Back to Savoy Festival and for a full weekend you will have the unique chance to learn from a true legend.

Maria Cossa

Maria is a dancer, a choreographer-exploer, idealist and a dreamer. She has been teaching African Dance since 2014 and she has been co-organizing The Day of Africa in Sofia in 2016, 2017 and 2018. In 2019 she spent 6 weeks at the international African Dance workshop in Senegal. She is also very well known for leading the series of workshops African Dance Expression in Sofia.

African dances are an integral part of the Harlem roots of Lindy Hop and Maria is joining the Back to Savoy Festival to help you explore the African Dance rhythms and vibrations.

Sebastian and the Others

Sebastian and the Others are a Sofia-based group of musicians playing swinging rhythms and tunes to get your feet moving and your body grooving! They will be the music stars of the festival.

DJ Miro and DJ Dimas

You might have probably met them - our local Swinging Jazz DJ duo - Miroslav and Dimitar. They will make sure that you have the top quality swinging tunes from the old day at all parties of the Back to Savoy festival.
